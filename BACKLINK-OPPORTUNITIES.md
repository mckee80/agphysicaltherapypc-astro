   - Website: https://www.aptapelvichealth.org/ptlocator
   - How: Must be an APTA member and Academy of Pelvic Health member
   - Cost: **Paid** (APTA membership ~$350/yr + Academy dues)
   - Notes: Extremely high authority in the pelvic PT niche. Patients actively search this directory.

2. **PelvicRehab.com (Herman & Wallace)** — Premier pelvic rehabilitation practitioner directory.
   - Website: https://pelvicrehab.com

   - Cost: **Paid** (course completion required; listing may be free after courses)
