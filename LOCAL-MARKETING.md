# Local Marketing & Community Trust Building

## Overview
Opportunities for AG Physical Therapy to build local trust and visibility in North County San Diego. Organized by category with priority ratings.

---

## 1. Referral Network Partnerships (Highest ROI)

These put Ashlee directly in front of women who need pelvic PT but may not know it exists.

| Partner Type | Opportunity | Action | Priority | Status |
|-------------|-------------|--------|----------|--------|
| **San Diego County Midwives** | Already sharing clinic space — deepen with co-hosted events, cross-referrals, joint content | Propose a joint workshop or Instagram Live | High | [ ] |
| **OB-GYN offices (North County)** | Lunch-and-learn for staff, leave brochures, become go-to PT referral | Identify 3-5 OB-GYN offices in Vista/Carlsbad/RB area | High | [ ] |
| **Doulas & birth workers** | San Diego has a large doula community; trusted by target demographic | Connect via SD Doula Association or local birth worker meetups | High | [ ] |
| **Lactation consultants** | Same postpartum audience, natural cross-referral | Identify local LCs and propose mutual referral arrangement | Medium | [ ] |
| **Chiropractors & acupuncturists** | Complementary (not competing) providers; cross-refer | Identify 2-3 in Vista/Carlsbad area | Medium | [ ] |
